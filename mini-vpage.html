Customization, Performance, and Genuine Parts for Your Mini

Mini is primarily known for its iconic and distinctive rounded design, bulldog profile, and endless customization options with interior and exterior colors and patterns. Here are several features that have drawn countless drivers to the marque.

Mini Key Features

Design Cues

Mini is known for its bold design cues emphasizing circular headlights, color customization options, and intriguing proportions with a more considerable distance between tires. Several customization options allow drivers to personalize everything from roof color to the dashboard.

Range of Engines

The Mini comes with a range of engine types, including 4-cylinder and turbocharged three-cylinder options. Its high-performance line, John Cooper Works (JCW), introduces much more potent engines not limited to your standard 1.5-liter turbocharged three-cylinder, including the 301-hp TwinPower Turbo, its most powerful engine to date.

Roomy Interior

Thanks to the Mini's unique wheel positioning and transverse engine layout, it allows for a roomier-than-average interior and ample storage options. Plus, highly adjustable seating, floor panels, and endless cargo accessories to keep your items organized and nearby.
