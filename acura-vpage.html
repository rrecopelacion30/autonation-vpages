Original Parts for Your Acura

Established in 1986, Acura is Japan’s first luxury auto brand. Off to a sluggish start in the North American market with the Legend and the Integra, it hit its stride in the late 1980s with the introduction of the NSX, a mid-engine sports car with an all-aluminum body that earned countless accolades for its innovation.

Here are three popular Acura models:

Acura MDX

The MDX is a midsize SUV with three-row seating, Super Handling All-Wheel Drive (SH-AWD), and the AcuraWatch Safety Suite on newer models. The ELS STUDIO 3D Premium Audio System, engineered by Grammy-winning artist Elliot Scheiner, is available with some packages.

Acura TLX

The TLX sedan offers one of Acura’s most impressive designs, with a bold Diamond Pentagon grille, sharp body lines, JewelEye LED headlights, and Chicane LED taillights for an aggressive design profile.

Acura RDX

The RDX is a performance-oriented crossover best known for its turbocharged engine, A-Spec sporty styling package, and a panoramic room moonroof that extends the entire cabin length.
